🎵 Rhyme 1 — We Are Pumpkins
We are pumpkins, big and round,
Big and round, big and round.
We are pumpkins, big and round,
Seated on the ground.
🌱 Rhyme 2 — A Little Seed
A little seed for me to sow,
A little earth to make it grow,
A little hole, a little pat,
A little wish, and that is that.
A little sun, a little shower,
A little while, and then a flower.
🫛 Rhyme 3 — Five Little Peas
Five little peas in the pea-pot pressed,
One grew, two grew and so did the rest,
They grew and they grew
and they did not stop
and so one day the pot went TUPP...!
🥕 Rhyme 4 — All Vegetables
All vegetables are good for me,
A...E...I...O...U
I like carrot and eat so much,
A...E...I...O...U
With a carrot here, and a carrot there,
Here a carrot, there a carrot,
Everywhere a carrot, carrot.
