Why do we need a House?
To protect us and keep us safe!

☔ Staying Safe

We all need a house to protect ourselves from Cold 🥶, Wind 💨, Rain 🌧️, and the hot Sun ☀️!

We live together with our family members. We share work and help each other. There is happiness in living together! 💕

🐾 Animals also need Houses!

👉 The 🐕 Dog lives in a Kennel.

👉 The 🐦 Bird lives in a Nest.

👉 The 🐄 Cow lives in a Shed.

👉 The 🐎 Horse lives in a Stable.

Rooms of a House
Each room is for a different work!

🛏️ We use different rooms!

1️⃣ Bathroom: Where we wash hands, legs, and face. 🛁

2️⃣ Kitchen: Where mother/father cooks food. 🍳

3️⃣ Hall / Living Room: Where we watch TV and chat. 📺

4️⃣ Bedroom: Where we sleep or study. 🛌

📍 Knowing Directions

Stand at your Main Door looking OUT!

👉 Things ahead of you are In front of you. 👆

👉 Things on your side can be on the Left side (👈) or Right side (👉).

👉 Things in the back of the house are Behind your house! 👇

Throwing the Waste
Keep your house neat and clean!
🌿 Wet Waste Bin

Put peels of vegetables, fruits, flowers, and leaves here. It can be used to prepare manure for plants!

📘 Dry Waste Bin

Put dry waste like paper pieces, plastics, and chocolate covers here!

"Don't throw the waste on the street! Give the waste every day to the person who collects it!" 🚛
