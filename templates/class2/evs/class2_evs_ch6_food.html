Where Does Food Come From?
We get food from plants and animals!

🌱 Food from Plants

Most of the food we eat comes from plants!

We get fruits 🍎, vegetables 🥕, and grains 🍚 from plants.

🐄 Food from Animals

We also get some yummy food from animals!

We get milk 🥛, eggs 🥚, and meat 🍗 from animals.

What Do Animals Eat?
Plant-eaters and Animal-eaters!

🌿 Plant Eaters

Some animals eat only plants and grass.

👉 Examples: Deer 🦌, Rabbit 🐇, Sheep 🐑, Cow 🐄.

🍗 Animal Eaters

Some animals eat other animals as their food!

👉 Examples: Tiger 🐅, Lion 🦁.

"Salim's cat 🐈 comes near him mewing. Salim gives the cat milk! 🥛
Develop the habit of feeding birds with grains! 🕊️"
