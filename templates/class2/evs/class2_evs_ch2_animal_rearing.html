Domestic Animals
Animals we rear at home!

🐄 What Are Domestic Animals?

Animals that are reared at home are called domestic animals. 🏡

👉 We give them food, water and shelter.

👉 They give us useful things like milk 🥛, eggs 🥚, and wool 🧶.

👉 Some animals live in our house but we don't rear them — like rats 🐀, lizards 🦎, and spiders 🕷️!

Wild Animals
Animals that live freely in the forest!

🦁 What Are Wild Animals?

Animals that live in the forest 🌲 are called wild animals.

👉 They find their own food and shelter.

👉 Nobody feeds them — they survive on their own!

👉 Lions 🦁, tigers 🐅, elephants 🐘, deer 🦌 — all are wild animals!

Caring for Animals
We rear animals with love, not just for use!

🤗 Why Do We Rear Animals?

We rear animals for their uses — milk, eggs, wool, guarding. But also to love and pet them! ❤️

👉 Kamala rears a rabbit 🐇 — she feeds it grass and pets it!

👉 Salim found a pigeon 🕊️ — he gives it grains and water!

👉 Lara has a pet cat 🐈 named Pintu — she gives him milk every day!

❤️ Animals are reared not only for use but also to love and pet them!
🏥 When animals fall ill, take them to the doctor (veterinarian)!

🛁 Keep domestic animals clean. 🥗 Give them proper food. 💊 Take them to a vet when sick. 🤗 Treat them with love!
