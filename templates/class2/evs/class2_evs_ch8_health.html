💧 Before and After Meals

2️⃣ Always wash hands AFTER taking meals!

Taking Care of Yourself
Nails, hair, and clothes!

💅 Keeping Clean

1️⃣ Always wear clean, washed clothes. 👕

2️⃣ After bath, comb your hair neatly. 🪮

3️⃣ Trim your nails at least once a week! Dirt hides inside long nails. ✂️

Evening & Night
After school and sleeping!

🎒 After School

When you come home from school, the first thing you should do is wash your hands, legs, and face! 🧼 Then, read your lessons daily! 📚

🛌 Time to Sleep!

Sleep in a well-ventilated room (windows let fresh air in 🌬️), and without covering your face!

"Cleanliness of the body is Health.
