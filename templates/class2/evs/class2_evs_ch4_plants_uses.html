Medicine
Plants can heal us!

💚 Medicine from Plants

When you have a cough or fever, some plants can make you feel better!

👉 Tulsi (Holy Basil): Good for curing severe cough and cold 🤧.

👉 Neem: Good for skin diseases and acting as an antiseptic 🍃.

👉 Lemon: Rich in Vitamin C, helps you fight fevers! 🍋

Ah! What a relief,

Other Useful Things!
Wood, Coir, Flowers, and Gum!

🥥 The Magic Coconut Tree!

Everything in the coconut tree is useful!

👉 Fruit: Tasty to eat! 🥥

👉 Tender water: Sweet to drink! 💧

👉 Dry Leaves: Used as firewood to light a fire! 🔥

👉 Sticks of leaves: Used to make a broom! 🧹

👉 Husk / Coir: Used to make mats and ropes! 🪢

🌺 Flowers (Jasmine, Lily) give sweet fragrance and are used for decoration!

🪵 We get wood from trees to construct houses, doors, and furniture!

👚 We get fibre from cotton plants to make clothes!
