When Seniors are Sick
Taking care of them!

🤒 Grandma has a Fever

Sometimes, grandmothers and grandfathers can get sick with a cough and fever.

When this happens, we should call a Doctor! 👨‍⚕️

Doctors will give them tablets (medicines) so they feel better soon! 💊

Helping Out
Lending a helping hand!

🚸 Helping in small ways

Oftentimes, elders might need our help with everyday tasks!

👉 If they say "I am feeling thirsty," we can give them a glass of water! 💧

👉 If it is difficult for them to cross the road, we can hold their hand and help! 🚶

Care and Love
We should respect our elders!
"While elders need our help and love, the sick need our care. You must learn to help and care, always be compassionate and fair." 💕
