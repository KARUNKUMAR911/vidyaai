Types of Plants
Trees, herbs, climbers & creepers!

🌟 Plants Come in Different Sizes!

Look around your backyard! You will see many plants and trees!

🌳 Trees — Big and tall plants. Example: Mango, Neem, Coconut!

🌿 Herbs — Small plants. Example: Tulsi, Mint!

🌱 Climbers — Plants that climb up with support. Example: Grape vine!

🥒 Creepers — Plants that spread on the ground. Example: Pumpkin, Watermelon!

Parts of a Plant
Root, trunk, branch, leaf, flower & fruit!

🌱 Every Plant Has Important Parts!

A plant has many parts. Each part does important work!

👉 Root — Grows under the ground. Holds the plant. Absorbs water. 💧

👉 Trunk (Stem) — Holds the plant upright. Carries water up! 🪵

👉 Branch — Arms of the tree! Holds leaves and fruits. 🌿

👉 Leaf — Makes food for the plant! Usually green. 🍃

👉 Flower — Beautiful and colourful! Becomes fruit! 🌺

👉 Fruit — Has seeds inside! We eat many fruits! 🍎
