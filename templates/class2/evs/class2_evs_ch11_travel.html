Land Transport
Vehicles that move on land!

🛣️ Moving on Roads and Tracks

The system of vehicles that move on land is called Land Transport.

Examples: Bus 🚌, Lorry 🚛, Car 🚗, Cycle 🚲, Motorbike 🏍️, Auto rickshaw 🛺, Horse cart 🐎, and Train 🚂!

Water Transport
Sailing across lakes and oceans!

🌊 Moving on Water

The system of vehicles that move on water is called Water Transport.

Examples: Boat ⛵, Ship 🚢, and Yacht 🚤!

They don't run on roads; they sail on water! 💧
