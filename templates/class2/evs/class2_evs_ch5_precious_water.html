Healthy Drinking Rules
Protect your health by keeping water clean!

🚰 How to handle Drinking Water?

1️⃣ Drinking water should always be clean!

2️⃣ The utensils to store water must be kept clean. 🏺

3️⃣ Use a cup with a long handle! Do not dip hands into the water. ✋❌

4️⃣ Keep the water container covered with a lid. 🛡️

5️⃣ Drinking boiled and cooled water is the best to kill germs! ♨️

Plants & Animals
They need water too!

🌱 Everyone Needs Water!

Human beings, animals, and plants ALL need water.

👉 If you don't water a plant, it will dry up and die! 🥀

👉 Animals drink water when they feel thirsty. 🐕💧

👉 Some plants and animals even live inside water! (Like fish 🐟 and lotus 🪷)

"We should save water without wasting. We should keep the water clean!" 💧
