Diwali
The Festival of Lights!

✨ Celebrating Diwali

Diwali is a festival celebrated by Hindus. It is the Festival of Lights!

All the members of the family wear new clothes and prepare a variety of sweets to eat! 🍬

Small lamps are lit at home, and green crackers are burst! 💥

Id-ul-Fither
Sharing happiness and sweets!

🌙 Ramzan Month

Id-ul-Fither is an important festival of the Muslims.

It is celebrated at the end of the Ramzan month!

Before celebrating, people observe one month of fasting. All family members distribute sweets and greet each other! 🤗

Christmas
Celebrated every December!

🎂 Birthday of Jesus

Christmas is the birthday of Jesus Christ!

It is celebrated every year on 25th December. 📅

People wear new dresses, distribute Cakes 🍰, and wish everyone "Merry Christmas!"
