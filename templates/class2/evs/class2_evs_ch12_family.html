🏡 Different kinds of Families

A family with the least members is called a Small Family.

A family with many members (like grandmother, grandfather, parents, and many siblings) is called a Large Family.

🧍 Differing Appearances

People look different! Some are tall and some are short.

Some have curly hair, some have straight hair!

Noses, ears, and skin colours are different too.

"We may be different for others to see, But we'll still love to play, Together for ever and ever to be!" 💕
