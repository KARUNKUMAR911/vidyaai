World of Insects!
Tiny creatures with 6 legs and feelers!

🐛 What Are Insects?

Have you seen an ant 🐜? Ants are insects!

👉 Insects have very small bodies.

👉 They have 6 legs (six legs!).

👉 They have a pair of feelers (antennae) on their head! 📡

👉 Mosquitoes 🦟, cockroaches 🪳, ants 🐜 — all are insects!

🦋 Common Insects
🐜Ant
🦟Mosquito
🪳Cockroach
🐝Honey Bee
🦗Grasshopper
🦋Butterfly
🐛Caterpillar
🪲Beetle
🐜 Insects = Small body + 6 legs + Feelers!
🦟 Mosquitoes and cockroaches have legs and feelers too!

🐝 Honey bees give us honey! 🍯 Butterflies help flowers grow! 🦋 But mosquitoes can spread diseases! 🦟

How Animals Move!
Walk, fly, swim, crawl, hop — every animal moves differently!

🌟 Different Ways of Moving

Animals move in different ways!

👉 🐕 Dogs and cats walk on 4 legs.

👉 🐦 Birds fly with their wings.

👉 🐟 Fish swim in water using fins.

👉 🐍 Snakes crawl on the ground.

👉 🐸 Frogs hop from place to place!
