ಸರಳರೇಖೆ — Straight Lines!
ಸರಳರೇಖೆ vs ವಕ್ರರೇಖೆ — Straight vs Curved

🌟 What is a Straight Line? (ಸರಳರೇಖೆ)

When you fold a paper once → you get 1 straight line! 📄➡️📏

When you fold a paper twice → you get 2 straight lines! 📄➡️📏📏

A tightly pulled thread also looks like a straight line! 🧵

A ruler, a wooden strip, and a book's edge can help draw straight lines!

ಅಡ್ಡರೇಖೆ
Horizontal
ಲಂಬರೇಖೆ
Vertical
ಓರೆರೇಖೆ
Slant

🌀 Curved Line (ವಕ್ರರೇಖೆ)

A loose thread becomes a curved line (ವಕ್ರರೇಖೆ)! 🧵

✅ Straight line = uses a ruler / folded paper

🌀 Curved line = bends and wiggles

Tight thread = Straight Line 📏
Loose thread = Curved Line 🌀
Joining 2 points = makes 1 Straight Line!

🌟 Geometric Properties (ಜ್ಯಾಮಿತೀಯ ಲಕ್ಷಣಗಳು)

🎳 Rolling (ಉರುಳುವುದು) — Objects with CURVED surfaces roll!

🛝 Sliding (ಜಾರುವುದು) — Objects with FLAT surfaces slide!

🎪 Some objects can do BOTH — roll AND slide!

⚽ Sphere (ಗೋಳ) → Only ROLLS (curved all over)

📦 Cuboid / Cube → Only SLIDES (flat surfaces)

🥫 Cylinder (ಸ್ತಂಭ) → Can ROLL and SLIDE both!

🪙 Coin (ನಾಣ್ಯ) → Can ROLL and SLIDE both!

Curved surface → ROLLS 🎳
Flat surface → SLIDES 🛝
Both curved + flat → ROLLS and SLIDES!

Edges (ಅಂಚು) and Corners (ಮೂಲೆ)

🌟 Edges and Corners!

Edge (ಅಂಚು) = the side/line of a shape

Corner (ಮೂಲೆ) = where two edges meet — a point!

✅ Objects with straight edges have corners!

🔴 Objects with only curved edges have NO corners!

🎲 Dice (Cube) → 12 Edges (ಅಂಚು) · 8 Corners (ಮೂಲೆ)

⚽ Ball (Sphere) → 0 Edges · 0 Corners

📦 Rubber (Cuboid) → 12 Edges · 8 Corners

🥫 Cylinder (ಸ್ತಂಭ) → curved edges, 0 corners

Objects with CORNERS have STRAIGHT edges! 🔺
Objects with only CURVED edges → NO corners! 🔵
ಮೂಲೆ = Corner · ಅಂಚು = Edge
