Addition — Mentally!
See how D'souza, Gowri & Salman think!

🎯 Example 1: What is 9 + 5?

Three friends tried different ways to solve 9 + 5 = ?
All three got the same answer: 14 🎉

Salman's trick was the fastest — he made 10 first!

👦 D'souza
Counted
dots 🔴🔴🔴
= 14
👧 Gowri
9+1+1+1+1+1
= 14
👦 Salman ⚡
9+1=10
10+4=14 ✅
🌟 Salman's FAST TRICK — "Make 10 First!"
9 + 5
= 9 + 1 + 4
= 10 + 4 = 14
Break the second number to make 10 first, then add the rest!

🎯 Example 2: What is 3 + 8?

D'souza thought: 3 + 8 = 1 + 2 + 8 = 1 + 10 = 11 ✅

When the second number is bigger, make 10 from that number instead!

💡 D'souza's trick for 3 + 8
3 + 8
= 1 + 2 + 8
= 1 + 10 = 11

🎯 Example 3: What is 7 + 6?

Gouri thought: 7 + 3 + 3 = 10 + 3 = 13 ✅

Split 6 into 3 + 3 to make 10 first!

💡 Gouri's trick for 7 + 6
7 + 6
= 7 + 3 + 3
= 10 + 3 = 13
⚡ The FAST TRICK:
Make 10 first, then add the rest!
This is the quickest mental addition method!

Subtraction — Mentally!
3 different tricks — pick what works for you!

🎯 Example 1: What is 8 − 5?

Three friends solved 8 − 5 = ? in different ways:

👩 Count down one by one: 8→7→6→5→4→3 = 3

👦 Subtract in steps: 8−3=5, 5−2=3 = 3

👧 Think addition: 8 = 5 + 3, so 8−5 = 3 ⚡

👩 Method 1
8→7→6→5
→4→3
= 3
👦 Method 2
8−3=5
5−2=3
= 3
👧 Method 3 ⚡
8=5+3
so 8−5
= 3 ✅
⚡ FASTEST TRICK:
Think of the addition fact!
If 5+3=8, then 8−5=3 ✅

🎯 Example 2: More practice!

👉 6 − 2 = 4 (think: 2 + 4 = 6)

👉 7 − 4 = 3 (think: 4 + 3 = 7)

👉 9 − 6 = 3 (think: 6 + 3 = 9)

🔁 Addition ↔ Subtraction are friends!
If 6 + 3 = 9
then 9 − 6 = 3 ✅
and 9 − 3 = 6 ✅

Multiples of Ten!
The Shathayushi Game + big number tricks!

🎮 The Shathayushi Game!

Children sit in a circle. One child says 10.
The next child adds 10 and says 20.
The game continues: 30, 40, 50... 100!

The child who says 100 is the Shathayushi 🏆
That child leaves the circle. Then it starts again from 10!

💡 The BIG SECRET for Multiples of 10!

To add multiples of 10, just add the tens digits, then put 0 at the end!

👉 50 + 40: think 5 + 4 = 9, so 50 + 40 = 90 ✅

👉 30 + 40: think 3 + 4 = 7, so 30 + 40 = 70 ✅

👉 60 + 20: think 6 + 2 = 8, so 60 + 20 = 80 ✅

🌟 The Pattern!
5 + 4 = 9
SO...
50 + 40 = 90 🎉
The single digit fact tells you the tens answer!
