Straight Lines
A line that does NOT bend is called a STRAIGHT line!

What is a Straight Line? 🤔

When you pull a string tight between two points — it makes a STRAIGHT LINE!
You can draw straight lines with a ruler, the edge of a book, or even a pencil box!

📏 ✏️ 📚

Folding paper ONCE gives you 1 straight line.
Folding paper TWICE gives you 2 straight lines!

Straight vs Curved Lines
A loose string makes a CURVED line!

Straight vs Curved 🧵

STRAIGHT line — string held TIGHT, goes directly from A to B.
CURVED line — string held LOOSE, bends and waves.

Letters like C, O, S, U have curved lines.
Letters like L, T, H, E have only straight lines.
Letters like B, D, P, R have both!

What Rolls? What Slides?
Objects have different surfaces — that decides how they move!

The 3 Rules 🧠

SLIDE — Objects with FLAT (plane) surfaces slide.
📚 Examples: Book, wooden plank, rubber (eraser)

ROLL — Objects with CURVED surfaces roll.
⚽ Examples: Ball, marble, orange

BOTH (Roll & Slide) — Objects with BOTH flat AND curved surfaces.
🪙 Examples: Coin, carrom pawn, cylinder

3D Shapes (Solid Shapes)
Cube · Cuboid · Cylinder · Cone · Sphere

Solid Shapes Around Us! 🏠

📦 Cuboid — like a brick, matchbox, or book. Has 6 rectangular faces.
🎲 Cube — like a dice or sugar cube. All 6 faces are SQUARES (equal).
🥫 Cylinder — like a tin can or battery. Has 2 circles + 1 curved surface.
🍦 Cone — like an ice cream cone or party hat. Pointed top!
⚽ Sphere — like a ball. Completely round & curved!

Edges & Corners
Flat edges, curved edges, corners & faces!

Edges vs Corners 🧠

EDGE — where two faces meet. Can be STRAIGHT or CURVED.
CORNER — where edges meet at a point (also called a vertex).

📦 A box (cuboid): 12 edges, 8 corners
🎲 A dice (cube): 12 edges, 8 corners
⚽ A ball (sphere): 0 edges, 0 corners

Objects with STRAIGHT edges HAVE corners.
Objects with only CURVED edges have NO corners!
