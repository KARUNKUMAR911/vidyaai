Addition Without Carry
Sum does not exceed 99. Units stay under 10!

How to Add: Tens & Units

When we add two numbers, we add the Units first, then the Tens.

🧮 Example: Rani has 24 beads. Mary has 32 beads.

Expanded Form Addition

We can break numbers into Tens and Units:
28 + 31 = 20 + 8 + 30 + 1
             = (20 + 30) + (8 + 1)
             = 50 + 9
             = 59

Addition With Carry Over
When units add up to 10 or more — carry the 1!

What is Carry Over?

Amith has 26 sticks. Shwetha has 38 sticks.

Step 1 — Add Units: 6 + 8 = 14
14 has 1 Ten and 4 Units.
Write 4 in Units place. Carry 1 to Tens!

Step 2 — Add Tens: 1 (carry) + 2 + 3 = 6

carry → ¹
2 6
+ 3 8
6 4

26 + 38 = 64 sticks! 🏆

💡 When Units add to 10 or more:
✅ Write the ones digit in the Units column.
✅ Carry 1 to the Tens column!

Another Example: 39 + 43

Units: 9 + 3 = 12 → write 2, carry 1
Tens: 1 + 3 + 4 = 8

¹
3 9
+ 4 3
8 2

39 + 43 = 82

Swap & Add
Change the order — the sum stays the same!

Addition by Interchanging Numbers

Veena asked Pavan: "2 + 3 = ?"
Pavan said: "5!"

Then Pavan asked: "3 + 2 = ?"
Veena said: "Also 5!"

🌟 3 + 2 = 2 + 3 = 5

Even though the numbers are swapped, the sum stays the same!

8 + 3 = 11
3 + 8 = 11
8 + 3 = 3 + 8 ✅

Three Numbers Too!

Example: 3 + 18 + 7 = 7 + 18 + 3
Both equal 28!

So: 22 + 13 = 13 + 22 = 35

Addition on Number Line
Jump forward to add! Sum not to exceed 9.

How to Use Number Line

Raziya jumped 3 steps and reached 3.
Then she jumped 2 more steps.
She reached 5! So 3 + 2 = 5.

🦘 Start at the first number.
🦘 Jump forward by the second number.
🦘 Where you land is the sum!

3 + 2 = 5 ✅

The number we add is called the Addend. The answer is the Sum.
Addend + Addend = Sum
