Yesterday, Today & Tomorrow
The day before = yesterday · The day after = tomorrow
If today is Monday 📅
Yesterday was Sunday ⬅️
Tomorrow will be Tuesday ➡️

🐰 Yesterday = the day BEFORE today
🐻 Today = right now!
🐇 Tomorrow = the day AFTER today

🔴 31-day months: Jan, Mar, May, Jul, Aug, Oct, Dec

🐸 A Leap Year has 366 days! It comes once every 4 years. In a leap year, February has 29 days!

Day and Night
Sunrise to Sunset = Day · Sunset to Sunrise = Night!
🌅
DAY TIME
☀️ We get sunlight
🦅 Birds fly
👷 We work
🌙
NIGHT TIME
🌟 We use artificial light
🐦 Birds rest in nests
😴 We sleep
🌅 Day begins with Sunrise
🌇 Day ends with Sunset
🌆 Night begins with Sunset
🌄 Night ends with Sunrise

🕐 In one full day, half the time is day and half the time is night!
