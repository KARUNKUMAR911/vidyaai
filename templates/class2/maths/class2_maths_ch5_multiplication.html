What is Multiplication?
Learn the big idea first!

🔁 Repeated Addition

When you add the same number again and again, that is called Repeated Addition.

Example: 2 + 2 + 2 = 6
Here, 2 is added 3 times.

✖️ Multiplication = Easy Repeated Addition

Multiplication is a short and easy way to write repeated addition.

Instead of writing 2 + 2 + 2, we write 2 × 3 = 6

The sign of multiplication is ×

Multiplicand × Multiplier = Product

2 × 6 = 12
2 is Multiplicand · 6 is Multiplier · 12 is Product

Key Words
Multiplicand · Multiplier · Product

🔑 The 3 Key Words

Multiplicand — the number being multiplied (first number)
Multiplier — the number of times we multiply (second number)
Product — the answer we get

Example: 2 × 6 = 12
Multiplicand = 2 · Multiplier = 6 · Product = 12

Any number multiplied by 0 gives 0
5 × 0 = 0  ·  9 × 0 = 0  ·  100 × 0 = 0
