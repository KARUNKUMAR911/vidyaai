Near and Far
Who is close? Who is far away?

🌟 Learn This!

Look at the picture: A boy and a girl are standing near a tree and a house.

👉 The girl is NEAR the house.

👉 The boy is FAR from the house.

👉 The boy is NEAR the tree.

👉 The girl is FAR from the tree.

NEAR = close to 🤏
FAR = away from 🏃

Long and Short
Which one stretches more?

A flute is LONG. 🎵

Tall, Short, High & Low
How high up? How tall is it?

🌟 Learn This!

A coconut tree is TALL. 🌴

A rose plant is SHORT. 🌹

A giraffe is TALLER than a rat. 🦒

A cow is TALLER than a hen. 🐄

Ambuja stands on the top floor — she is HIGH! 🏢

Girija stands on the ground floor — she is LOW! 🏠

An aeroplane in the sky is HIGH. ✈️

A train on the ground is LOW. 🚂

TALL/SHORT = height of objects 🌴🌹
HIGH/LOW = how high from the ground ✈️🚂
Write T for Tall · S for Short

Non-Standard Measuring Units
Measure with your body and everyday things!

🌟 Non-Standard Units of Length

Before rulers were invented, people used their bodies to measure!

🦶1 Foot
👋1 Span
💪1 Cubit
🤏4 Fingers
🚶1 Pace
🙆1 Fathom

🎮 Gilli-Dandu: 1 Dandu = 3 Gillis! Children use gilli to measure SHORT lengths and dandu to measure LONG lengths.

🎮 Marble Game: Children measure the distance between marbles using hand span!

Short length → use small unit (fingers, span)
Long length → use big unit (dandu, fathom, pace)
