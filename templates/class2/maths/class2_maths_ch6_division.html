What is Division?
Learn the big idea first!

🤝 Equal Sharing

When you share things equally among friends, that is called Division!

Example: You have 6 mangoes and 3 friends.
Give 2 mangoes each — everyone gets the same!

➗ The Division Sign

Division is a short and easy way to write equal sharing.

Instead of sharing one by one, we write: 6 ÷ 3 = 2

The sign of division is ÷

Dividend ÷ Divisor = Quotient

6 ÷ 3 = 2
6 is Dividend · 3 is Divisor · 2 is Quotient

Activity 2 — Sharing Chocolates
See how equal sharing works step by step!

There are 9 Chocolates in a plate.
Distribute them equally among 3 children.
How many chocolates will each get?

🔄 Share One by One

We give 1 chocolate at a time to each child, until all chocolates are shared!

Round 1
9 − 3 = 6
Give 1 chocolate to each child → 6 chocolates remain
Round 2
6 − 3 = 3
Give 1 more to each child → 3 chocolates remain
Round 3
3 − 3 = 0
Give 1 more to each child → 0 remain! All shared! ✅
9 ÷ 3 = 3
Each child gets 3 chocolates! 🍫🍫🍫
