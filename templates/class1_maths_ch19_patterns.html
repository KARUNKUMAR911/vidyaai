Things that REPEAT in order!

Thumb & Finger Patterns!
Stamping with YOUR hands!
👍 Thumb Stamping!
Press your thumb in paint and stamp on paper!

One thumb stamp = 1
Two thumb stamps = 2

Arrange them in a sequence to make a pattern!

🤚 Hand Stamping!
Even your whole HAND can make a pattern stamp!

Press your palm in paint → stamp on paper → you see your 5 fingers!

Each hand stamp looks exactly the same — that's a repeating pattern!
