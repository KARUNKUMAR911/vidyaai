Measurement
How do we measure length?
📖 What is Measurement?
We can compare the length of two things by keeping one beside the other.

But to know HOW LONG something is, we MEASURE it!

We use our body parts to measure — this is called using non-standard units.
🦶
Cubit · Hand Span · Foot Span
Three ways to measure length!
🦾
Cubit
From your elbow to the tip of your middle finger.
Used by Lakshmi to measure a garland = 1 cubit long.
🖐️
Hand Span
Spread your hand wide — from thumb tip to little finger tip.
Used by Gowri: garland = 2 hand spans long.
John measured a TV = 5 hand spans.
🦶
Foot Span
The length of your foot from heel to toe.
Used by Ramu: stick = 5 foot spans long.
Asha measured classroom = 18 foot spans.
