What happens first? Tap the answer!
🔑 Key Words
EARLIER LATER

Raju wakes up in the morning — this happens EARLIER. 🌅
Raju goes to sleep at night — this happens LATER. 🌙

Raju has breakfast — this is EARLIER. 🍽️
Raju plays football in the evening — this is LATER. ⚽

Something that happens first is called EARLIER.
Something that happens after is called LATER.

Day and Night
Sun shines in the day! Stars shine at night!
🔑 Key Words
DAY NIGHT

When the sun shines it is DAY. ☀️
When the moon and stars come out it is NIGHT. 🌙⭐

We go to school during the DAY. 🎒
We play outside during the DAY. ⚽

We go to sleep during the NIGHT. 😴
The sky is bright in the DAY and dark in the NIGHT.
