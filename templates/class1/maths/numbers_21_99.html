Tens and Units!
How many TENS? How many UNITS?
💡 Tens and Units!
Every big number has TENS (bundles of 10) and UNITS (single ones)!

26 → 2 TENS + 6 UNITS
43 → 4 TENS + 3 UNITS
80 → 8 TENS + 0 UNITS

The TENS digit is on the left. The UNITS digit is on the right!
Tap the correct TENS or UNITS!

Forward & Backward!
Fill in the missing numbers on the train!
💡 Counting!
Forward counting → numbers go UP: 21, 22, 23, 24 …
Backward counting → numbers go DOWN: 30, 29, 28, 27 …

Each number is 1 more (forward) or 1 less (backward) than the one before!
Tap the missing number!

Match the Number!

💡 Before · After · Between!
Comes before 52 → the number just before it = 51
Comes after 63 → the number just after it = 64
Comes in between 51 and 53 → the number between = 52
