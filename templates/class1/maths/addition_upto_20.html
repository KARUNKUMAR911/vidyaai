📖 What is Addition?
When we put two groups together we add them! The answer is called the sum! The sum here is not more than 20!
Tap the correct sum!
🔢
Count and Add!
Count the objects, then tap the sum!
💡 How to Count and Add!
Count the objects in Group 1. Then count the objects in Group 2. Now put them together and count them all! That is your sum!
Count all and tap the answer!
📦
Tens and Units!
Group 10 to make a TEN!
💡 Tens and Units!
When we add numbers and get more than 9, we make a group of 10 (one TEN)! The ones left over are the UNITS!
Example: 7 + 4 = 11 = 1 TEN 1 UNIT
Example: 8 + 5 = 13 = 1 TEN 3 UNITS
Tap the correct sum!
🔗
Group and Add!
Group the lines into Tens and Units!
💡 Group and Add!
Draw lines (tally marks) for each number. Group 10 lines together to make 1 TEN. The remaining lines are UNITS! Count tens and units to find the sum!
Find the Tens and Units, then the sum!

Add and Match!
Find equal sums — match the pairs!
💡 Match the equal sums!
Two different additions can give the same answer! For example: 5 + 8 = 13 and 9 + 4 = 13 — they are equal! Find the pairs!
Tap the addition that gives the same answer!
