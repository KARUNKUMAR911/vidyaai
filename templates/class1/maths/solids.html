Solids Around Us!
Objects have different SHAPES. Let's learn them!
What is a SOLID?
Everything around us has a shape. A ball is round. A box is a cube. A can is a cylinder. These are called SOLIDS!

Roll & Slide!
Different shapes move in different ways!
⚽
ROLLING
Round objects ROLL! (sphere, cylinder)
📦
SLIDING
Flat objects SLIDE! (cube, cuboid)
🎯 The Secret Rule!
ROUND objects → ROLL (Sphere, Cylinder, Cone can roll!)
FLAT-SIDED objects → SLIDE (Cube, Cuboid slide!)
Some shapes like Cylinder can BOTH roll AND slide!
✅ Tap objects that ROLL!
🖍️ Tap objects that SLIDE!

Flat Shapes!
Top surfaces show us flat 2D shapes!
2D = FLAT Shapes!
When we look at the top of an object, we see a flat shape! A coin top → Circle. A box top → Square or Rectangle. A sandwich → Triangle!
🔴
CIRCLE
Perfectly round, no corners!
🔺
TRIANGLE
3 sides, 3 corners!
🟦
RECTANGLE
2 long + 2 short sides!
🟧
SQUARE
4 equal sides!
What flat shape is on top? Tap the correct answer! 👇
