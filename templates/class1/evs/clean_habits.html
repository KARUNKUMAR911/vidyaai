Clean Habits!
Good habits keep us healthy and happy!
🧼
Cleanliness keeps GERMS away and keeps us HEALTHY!
🪥 What Are Clean Habits?
Clean habits are things we do every day to keep our body clean! 🧼

When we are clean, we stay healthy and do not fall sick! 💪

We must do these habits in the morning, evening and night! 🌙
☀️ Morning Habits!
1 ☀️ Brush your teeth every morning! 🪥
2 🚿 Take a bath with clean water! 🛁
3 👗 Wear clean and neat clothes! 👕
4 💇 Comb your hair neatly! 🪮
🍽️ Before & After Eating!
🙌 Wash hands with soap BEFORE eating!
🙌 Wash hands AFTER eating too!
💅 Cut your nails regularly with a nail cutter! ✂️
🏠 After Coming Home!
🏡 Wash your hands and feet when you come home from school!
👟 Keep your shoes and bag in the right place!
🤔 Quick Think!
What do you do FIRST when you wake up in the morning?
What do you do BEFORE eating your breakfast? 🍳
