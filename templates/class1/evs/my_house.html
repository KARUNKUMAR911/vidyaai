This Is My House!
Every person lives in a house!
🏠
Our house is where we LIVE, EAT, SLEEP and feel SAFE!
🏠 What Is a House?
A house is a place where we live with our family! 👨‍👩‍👧

A house has walls, a roof, doors and windows! 🚪🪟

We sleep, eat, play and stay safe in our house! 😊
🚪 Parts of a House!
🚪Door — we enter and go out through the door!
🪟Window — windows let in light and fresh air!
🏠Roof — the roof protects us from rain and sun!
🧱Walls — walls make our house strong and safe!
🏡Yard — some houses have a yard outside to play!

Near — a little walk away! 🚶🚶
Far — you need to walk more! 🚶🚶🚶
