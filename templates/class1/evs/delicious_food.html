What Do We Eat?
We eat many different and delicious foods every day!
🌍 Food Is Available in Our Locality!
We eat food every single day! 🌟

Food gives us energy ⚡ to play, run and study.

Different places have different types of food. Let's learn about the delicious food available in our locality! 😋
🎯 Lesson Goal: Know the food available in your locality 🏘️
Identify food grains grown near you 🌾
Know the food which you eat every day! 🍴
🍛 Popular Foods We Eat
🍚Idli — soft, steamed rice cakes made from rice and dal!
🫓Roti / Chapati — flat bread made from wheat flour!
🍳Dosa — thin, crispy pancake made from rice and dal!
🍱Rice — cooked rice eaten with dal, sambar or curry!
🥣Poha / Chitranna — flattened rice dish made with spices!
🍞Bread — made from wheat, eaten as toast or sandwich!
🐟Fish — caught from rivers and seas, cooked in many ways!
🥚Eggs — boiled, scrambled or used in cooking!
🥛Milk — from cows and buffaloes, very healthy to drink!
🍮Curd — made from milk, eaten with rice or as lassi!
🎊 Festival Special Foods!
On special days and festivals we make extra-special dishes!

🪔 Diwali — sweets and ladoos! 🍬
🌾 Sankranti — til pongal and ellu bella! 🍯
🎉 Ugadi — bevu bella! 🌿
🕌 Eid — biryani and sewain! 🍚
⛪ Christmas — cake and plum pudding! 🎂

Food Grains & Ingredients!
Grains, fruits, vegetables and more!
🌾 What Are Food Grains?
Food grains are the seeds of plants that we eat! 🌱

Farmers grow food grains in their fields 🌾.
They harvest them and bring them to our homes.

Rice, wheat and ragi are some food grains grown in Karnataka! 🏡
🍚
Rice
Grown in water-logged fields!
🌾
Wheat
Makes roti and bread!
🟤
Ragi
Makes mudde and roti!
🌽
Corn / Maize
Can be eaten roasted or boiled!
🥬 Vegetables in Our Locality
🍆Brinjal (Baingan) — purple vegetable, made into curry!
🥕Carrot — orange, can eat raw or cooked in sabzi!
🍅Tomato — red, used in almost every dish!
🧅Onion — adds taste to all curries!
🥦Okra (Lady's finger) — green, eaten as stir-fry!
🥔Potato — used in so many dishes everywhere!
🫚Radish (Mooli) — white root vegetable, eaten raw!
🍎 Fruits We Love
🍌Banana — sweet, easy to peel and eat anytime!
🥭Mango — the king of fruits! Sweet and juicy! 🤩
🍇Grapes — small, sweet and juicy little fruits!
🍎Apple — red and crunchy, very healthy!
🍈Papaya — orange inside, sweet and healthy!
🥛 Other Food Items
🥛Milk — from cows and buffaloes! Builds strong bones!
🍮Curd (Mosaru) — made from milk, eaten with rice!
🐟Fish — from rivers and sea, good source of protein!
🥩Meat — chicken, mutton eaten as curry or fry!
🫚Ginger — root used as spice to add taste to food!

Raw Food & Cooked Food!
Some foods we eat as-is, some need cooking!
🌿 Two Types of Food!
Some foods can be eaten raw — just wash and eat! 🥗

Some foods must be cooked before eating — we use fire to cook them! 🔥

Food & What It's Made From!
Every dish needs special ingredients!
🍳 From Grain to Dish!
Your book shows a fun activity where the outer circle has the food we eat, and the inner circle has the grains and ingredients used to make them!

Let's learn what each dish is made from! 🌾➡️🍽️
🔗 Match: Food ↔ What It's Made From!
🍚 Rice ← 🌾 Rice grain (chawal)
🫓 Roti / Chapati ← 🌾 Wheat
🍚 Idli ← 🍚 Rice + Urad dal
🟡 Chitranna / Poha ← 🍚 Flattened rice (poha)
🍳 Dosa ← 🍚 Rice + Urad dal
🥣 Ragi Mudde ← 🟤 Ragi (finger millet)
🍮 Curd (Mosaru) ← 🥛 Milk
🍲 Dal / Sambar ← 🟤 Lentils (dal/masoor)
