🌟 Our 3 Heavenly Friends!
In the sky we can see THREE special friends:

☀️ The SUN
🌙 The MOON
✨ The STARS

They are called our HEAVENLY FRIENDS because they live in the sky (heaven) above us! 🌈
☀️
Sun
Shines in the DAY! Gives us light and warmth ☀️
🌙
Moon
Seen at NIGHT! Glows softly in the dark sky 🌙
⭐
Stars
Tiny dots in the NIGHT sky! Very far away ✨
📍 Where are they?
The Sun, Moon and Stars are all in the SKY above us!
They are very, very FAR AWAY from the Earth! 🌍
☀️ SUN + 🌙 MOON + ⭐ STARS
= Our 3 HEAVENLY FRIENDS in the SKY!

☀️ The SUN is our Daytime Friend!
During the DAY, we see the SUN in the sky!

☀️ The Sun is very BIG and BRIGHT!
☀️ It gives us LIGHT during the day!
☀️ It gives us WARMTH (heat)!
☀️ Without the Sun, the day would be DARK and COLD!

The day looks BRIGHT and GAY because the Sun is here! 🌈
☀️
Here comes the Sun!
With him he brings some fun! The day now looks so bright and gay. We only want to sing and play! 🎵

When the Sun SETS (goes down), it gets dark!
Night begins! 🌙
🌅 What happens when the Sun sets?
When the Sun goes down (sets):

🌇 The sky gets DARK!
🌙 The MOON appears!
⭐ The STARS come out!
🌃 It becomes NIGHT!
☀️ DAY = SUN in the sky!
🌙 When Sun sets = NIGHT begins!
MOON and STARS come out at night!

🌙 The MOON — Our Night Friend!
At NIGHT, we see the MOON in the sky!

🌙 The Moon gives us soft, gentle light at night!
🌙 The Moon looks like a big curved shape (crescent)!
🌙 The Moon changes its shape every few days!
🌙 We can see the Moon clearly on a dark night!
⭐ The STARS — Tiny Dots of Light!
At night we also see many STARS!

⭐ Stars are very far away from the Earth!
⭐ Because they are so far, they look like tiny dots!
⭐ They are actually very big — like the Sun!
⭐ We can count the stars with our friends! 🤝
⭐ Stars twinkle twinkle in the night sky! ✨
