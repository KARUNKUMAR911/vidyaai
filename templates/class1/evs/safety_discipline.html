What is Danger?
Things that can hurt us!
⚠️
Some things around us can HURT us! We must know them and STAY AWAY!
⚠️ What is Danger?
Danger means something that can hurt us or cause harm! 😨

Things like electricity, fire, sharp objects, and busy roads are dangerous!

We must always be careful and safe! 🛡️

✅ Safe Things to Do!
🚶Always walk on the footpath — not on the road!
🧑‍🦱Listen to your parents, elders and teachers!
🏠Stay away from electric switches and sockets!
🔥Never play near fire or hot objects!
🧗Do not climb walls, trees or high places alone!
✂️Never play with sharp scissors, knives or pins!

Standing in a Queue!
Wait for your turn — be disciplined!
🚌
A QUEUE means standing in a line and waiting for your TURN!
🚌 What is a Queue?
A queue is a line of people waiting for their turn! 🧑‍🤝‍🧑

We stand in a queue at the bus stop, post office, water tap, school and many places!

Standing in a queue is a sign of good discipline! 🌟
🚌 At the Bus Stop!
🚌
🧒👦👧👩🧑👴👵
Everyone waits in a line — one by one! ✅
📋 Where Do We Stand in a Queue?
🚌 At the Bus Stop — to get into the bus!
📮 At the Post Office — to post letters!
💧 At the Water Tap — to fill water pots!
🏫 At School — to collect books and food!
💡 Why is Queue Important?
😊All people get their turn fairly!
💧All will get sufficient water if we wait for our turn!
✅There will be NO problems if we follow the queue!
🌟Waiting in a queue shows good manners!
🤔 Think!
