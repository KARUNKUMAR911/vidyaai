Keep It Clean!
A clean neighbourhood is a happy neighbourhood!
🧹
We must SWEEP and CLEAN our neighbourhood to keep it TIDY!
📖 Story Time: Dirt in Front!
One day a child saw DIRT in front of the house 😟

The child called the neighbour: "Kamala, come let's sweep!" 🧹

Together they swept the front of the house — now it looks CLEAN! ✨
🏠
Before — Dirty! 😟
There is DIRT in front of the house. It looks very messy and bad.
🧹
After — Clean! ✨
Kamala and the child swept together. Now it looks CLEAN! ✅
📖 Story Time: Water Near the Tap!
There was WATER all around the tap 💦 It was very dirty and wet!

Neighbours worked together and cleaned the area near the tap 🧹

Now the place near the tap is CLEAN! ✨
🚰
Problem — Water Everywhere! 💦
There is water around the tap. The place looks dirty and slippery.
✅
Solution — Now It Looks Clean! ✅
Neighbours cleaned together. The place near the tap is now neat and dry!
🧹 Sweeping keeps our home front CLEAN!
🚰 Cleaning near the tap stops dirt & germs!
A clean neighbourhood = a HEALTHY neighbourhood! 💚
