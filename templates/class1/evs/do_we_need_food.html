Why Do We Need Food?
Food makes us healthy and strong!
💪
We need food to be HEALTHY and STRONG!
🌟 What Does Food Do For Us?
Just like a car needs petrol to run, our body needs food to work! 🚗⛽

Without food we feel tired, weak and sad 😔

With food we feel bright, strong and happy 😄✨
🍽️ Food Helps Us To...
⚡ Get ENERGY to play, run and jump all day!
📚 THINK clearly and study well at school!
💪 Build STRONG muscles and healthy bones!
🌱 GROW tall and big like a healthy tree!
🛡️ PROTECT us from getting sick and ill!
😊 Stay HAPPY, bright and cheerful always!
🎵 Sing and Enjoy!

before I went to bed.
He seemed so tired without food
And this is what I said,
"Go friend eat your dinner right
And see you'll sure feel bright".
He then ate rice and dal and grains.
Which made him bright and strong! 💪
🎯 Remember:
Without food → tired and weak 😔
With food → bright and strong! 💪😄
