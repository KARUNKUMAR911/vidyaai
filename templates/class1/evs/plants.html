Plants Are All Around Us!
In our houses, schools, fields and roadsides!
👀 What Are Plants?
Plants are living things that grow from the soil 🌱.

They give us fruits 🍎, vegetables 🥦, flowers 🌸 and oxygen to breathe! 💨

We can see plants everywhere — in our gardens, on roadsides, in fields and in forests!

Trees, Shrubs & Herbs!
Plants come in different sizes and types!
🌳 Big Plants — TREES
Trees are very tall and have a thick woody trunk. They live for many many years! 🌲
🌳 Trees (Tall, thick trunk)
🥥 Coconut 🥭 Mango 🌴 Palmyra (Toddy palm) 🫙 Jackfruit 🌴 Bamboo 🍎 Pomegranate 🍐 Guava
🌿 Medium Plants — SHRUBS
Shrubs are smaller than trees. They have many thin branches growing from near the ground! 🌿
🌿 Shrubs (Medium, many branches)
🌹 Rose 🌼 Marigold 🌸 Hibiscus (Jasmine) 🌺 Dahlia 🌼 Daisy (Chamomile) 🌱 Tulsi (Holy Basil)
🌱 Small Plants — HERBS & CLIMBERS
Herbs are small, soft plants. Climbers use other things to climb up! 🍇
🌱 Herbs & Climbers (Small, soft stems)
🌶️ Chilli 🍅 Tomato 🌽 Maize (Corn) 🎃 Pumpkin 🍇 Grapes 🥒 Cucumber 🌾 Paddy (Rice) 🥜 Groundnut

Fruits, vegetables, flowers and grains!
