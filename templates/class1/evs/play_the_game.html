Games We Play!
We play many different games!
🎉
We play MANY games — indoor games and outdoor games!
🌳 Outdoor Games
We play these games OUTSIDE in the open!
We need space, fresh air, and energy! 💪
🏷️
Blind Fold
One person is blindfolded, others run away!
🪀
Marbles
We flick small marbles to hit others!
🪢
Spinning Top
Wind the string and spin the top fast!
🪙
Goli Shells
We play with shells on the ground!
🏃
Kabaddi
Hold your breath and tag others!
🤸
Chain Game
Hold hands and catch others in a chain!
🏠 Indoor Games
We play these games INSIDE the house!
No big space needed — just fun! 😄
🎯
Hopscotch
Jump on squares drawn on the ground!
🃏
Carrom
Flick coins into the corner pockets!
🔢
Number Board
Play number games on a grid board!
🐥
Duck-Duck-Goose
Sit in a circle — "Duck, duck, GOOSE!"
🏟️ Big Sports Games
These are big games played in teams!
They are also played in big stadiums! 🏆
🏏
Cricket
Hit the ball with a bat — run for runs!
⚽
Football
Kick the ball into the goal!
🏀
Basketball
Throw the ball into the basket!
🤼
Kabaddi
Tag players without breathing out!
🏐
Volleyball
Hit the ball over the net!
🏑
Lagori
Stack stones and knock them down!
🌟 Playing games is GOOD for us!
We become STRONG 💪 and HEALTHY 😊
We make FRIENDS and learn to be a TEAM! 🤝

Game Tools!
Things we use to play games!
🎯
Every game uses DIFFERENT things to play!
🔍 Which game uses what?
Look at the tool — which game do we use it in? Let's learn!
🪢
Spinning Top (Lattu)
We wind a string around the SPINNING TOP and throw it on the ground. It spins fast!
⚽
Football
We use a big FOOTBALL and kick it into the goal. Two teams play!
🪢
Skipping Rope
We hold a long ROPE and jump over it again and again. It is great exercise!
🐚
Goli (Shells)
We use small SHELLS or STONES to play Goli on the ground!
🏏
Cricket Bat & Ball
We use a CRICKET BAT to hit the ball and score runs for the team!
🃏
Carrom Board
We use a CARROM BOARD with coins and a striker. Flick the coins into the pockets!
🏏 Bat & Ball → Cricket!
⚽ Football → Football game!
🃏 Carrom Board → Carrom!
🪢 Rope → Skipping!
🪢 String → Spinning Top!

Seven Days, Seven Games!

And Sunday is a holiday.

On a Monday, I catch a ball 🏏
On a Tuesday, I hit a goal ⚽

I swing up high on a Wednesday 🪁
I scream Lagori on a Thursday 🏑

On a Friday, I basket the ball 🏀
On a Saturday, I play Kabaddi 🤼

Only seven days is a week,
Not enough for me to play! 😄
