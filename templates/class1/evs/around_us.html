🌊 The SEA — So Big and Blue!
The SEA is a very large body of salt water!

🌊 The sea is huge and deep!
🚢 Big ships sail on the sea!
🐟 Many fish and sea animals live in the sea!
🏖️ Beaches are found near the sea — with golden sand!

We can see a boat floating on the peaceful blue sea! ⛵
🏞️ The RIVER — Flowing Water!
A RIVER is flowing water that moves through the land!

🌿 Rivers flow through forests and mountains!
🐟 Fish live in rivers!
💧 Rivers give us fresh water to drink!
🌾 Farmers use river water to grow crops!
💧
Waterfall
Water falling down from a height! Very beautiful! 🏔
🏗️
Dam
A wall built to hold river water! Gives electricity! ⚡
💧 Waterfall and Dam!
WATERFALL — Water falls from a great height over rocks! It looks amazing!

DAM — A big wall built across a river to store water! The water stored in a dam is used for:
⚡ Making electricity!
💧 Drinking water!
🌾 Watering crops!
🌊 SEA (salty, huge) + 🏞️ RIVER (flowing) + 💧 WATERFALL + 🏗️ DAM
= Water around us!

Places Around Us!
Village, School, Factory and more!
🏡 A Village!
A VILLAGE is a small place where people live!

🏠 There are small houses in a village!
🌳 There are many trees around!
🌾 Farmers live and work in villages!
🏗️ There is often a well (🪣) to get water!
🐄 Animals like cows roam freely!

A village is a very peaceful and beautiful place! 🌿
🪣
Well
A deep hole in the ground to get water! 💧
🏫
School
Govt. H.P.S. — where children learn! 📚
🏭
Factory
Makes things! Has tall chimneys with smoke! 🏗️
🏭 A Factory!
A FACTORY is a big building where things are made!

🏗️ Factories have big machines!
💨 They have tall chimneys that make smoke!
⚠️ Too much smoke from factories makes the air DIRTY (pollution)!
🌿 We must take care that factories do not pollute our air!
🌿
Our locality has MANY things!
Some are natural, some are man-made! 🏡

📍 What is a Locality?
A LOCALITY is the place where YOU live!

🏠 It is your neighbourhood — the area around your home!
🌳 Your locality may have trees, roads, shops, schools!
🌊 Some localities are near sea, rivers or mountains!

Every locality is DIFFERENT and SPECIAL! 🌟
