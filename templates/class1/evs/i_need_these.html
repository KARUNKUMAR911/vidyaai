🌟 What are BASIC NEEDS?
Things we MUST have to live are called our BASIC NEEDS!
We cannot survive without our basic needs! 💪

Which ones do we REALLY need every day?
Let's find out together! 👇

People Need!
What do people need to live?
👨‍👩‍👧
ALL people — big and small — need the SAME basic things!
📖 Listen to what the children say!
Just like in the book, children tell us what they need every day!
👧
🍽️ "I am hungry.
Can we have FOOD?"
🧒
💧 "Oh! I am thirsty.
I need WATER to drink."
👦
🏠 "It started raining.
Let's go HOME."
👦
👕 "I need CLOTHES to protect me from heat, cold, rain and breeze."
👧
💨 "We need AIR to breathe also. Don't we?"
🌟 Our 5 Basic Needs!
The lesson tells us these are our basic needs!
💨
Air
We breathe air every second! Cannot live without it!
💧
Water
We drink water when thirsty. We need it to live!
🍽️
Food
We eat food when hungry. Food gives us energy!
👕
Clothes
Clothes protect from sun, cold, rain and breeze!
🏠
House
A house keeps us safe from rain and cold! We sleep and live in it!
📚 The lesson says:
"All need Air, Water, Clothes, House.
These are our BASIC NEEDS." 🌟

Animals Need!
Animals have needs too!
🐘
The lesson says: "Animals also need AIR, WATER and FOOD!"
🐄 Animals from the book!
The book shows us these animals — all of them need air, water and food to live!
🐄
Cow
Eats grass 🌿, drinks water 💧, breathes air 💨
🐘
Elephant
Eats leaves 🍃, drinks lots of water 💧, breathes air 💨
🐃
Buffalo
Grazes on grass 🌿, needs water 💧 and air 💨
🐎
Horse
Drinks from rivers 🌊, eats hay, breathes air 💨
🐑
Sheep (Lamb)
Grazes on grass 🌿, needs water 💧 and air 💨
🐕
Dog
Eats food 🦴, drinks water 💧, breathes air 💨
🤔 What do ALL animals need?
💨 Air — to breathe!
💧 Water — to drink!
🍽️ Food — to eat and get energy!

Whether it's a cow, elephant, horse or dog — they ALL need the same three things!
🐄 Cow • 🐘 Elephant • 🐃 Buffalo • 🐎 Horse • 🐑 Sheep • 🐕 Dog
ALL animals need: 💨 Air + 💧 Water + 🍽️ Food!
🌱
Plants Need!
Plants are living things too!
🌿
The lesson says: "Plants also need AIR, WATER and FOOD!"
🌞 What do Plants need?
Just like us and animals, plants are living things!
They also need air, water and food to grow and stay alive! 🌱
💧
Water
We water plants so they can drink! Just like we get thirsty!
☀️
Sunlight
Plants use sunlight to make their own food! So clever! 🌞
💨
Air
Plants breathe in air through tiny holes in their leaves!
🌍
Soil (Food)
Plants get food from the soil through their roots!
👧
Watering the Plant 💧
In the book, a girl waters a plant with a watering can. She gives it water so it can grow strong! 🌱
👦
Adding Soil 🌍
In the book, a boy adds soil around the plant. Soil gives the plant food (nutrients) to grow big! 🌿
🌟 Everyone needs the SAME things!
👨‍👩‍👧 People need → Air, Water, Food, Clothes, House
🐄 Animals need → Air, Water, Food
🌱 Plants need → Air, Water, Food (from soil + sun)

Air, Water and Food are needed by ALL living things! 🌍
🌱 Plants need: 💨 Air + 💧 Water + ☀️ Sunlight + 🌍 Soil!
Plants are ALIVE — they have needs too! 🌿
