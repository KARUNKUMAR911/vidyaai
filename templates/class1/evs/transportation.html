Water Transport!
Vehicles that travel on water!
⛵
Water transport travels on RIVERS, LAKES and SEAS! Boats and ships float on water!
🌊 Water Transport
Water transport uses rivers, lakes, and seas!

A boat 🛥️ is rowed using oars! The oars splash in the water — splash splash splash!

Bigger water vehicles include ships and steamers!

We use water transport to cross rivers and travel to islands!

Air Transport!
Vehicles that fly in the sky!
✈️
Air transport travels in the SKY! Aeroplanes fly swish swish swish high above the clouds!
✈️ Air Transport
Air transport uses the sky! ☁️

The aeroplane ✈️ is the fastest way to travel!
Its wings fly — swish swish swish!

Aeroplanes take off from an airport!

We can reach very far away places quickly by aeroplane!
