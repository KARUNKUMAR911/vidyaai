The Thirsty Crow!
Salim is thirsty. He is drinking water.
💧 What is Water?
Water is the most important thing in the world! 🌍

All living things — people 🧑, animals 🐄, and plants 🌱 — need water to stay alive!

When we are thirsty, we drink water. Just like Salim! 🥤
📖 The Crow and the Pot — Look and Say the Story!
Picture 1️⃣
🐦
A crow is flying. It is very thirsty!
Picture 2️⃣
🏺
The crow sees a pot. The water is very low!
Picture 3️⃣
🪨
The crow drops stones into the pot!
Picture 4️⃣
💧
The water comes up! The crow drinks it! 😊
❓ Why does the crow drop stones?
👉 To make the water RISE UP so it can drink! 🐦💧

Plants & Animals Need Water Too!
All living things need water to survive!
📘 Your Textbook Says:
"Even plants, trees and animals need water like us." 🌱🐄💧
🌱 Plants Need Water!
Remember the picture in your book? A plant was drying up 😟.

Then a girl watered it 💧... and the plant became fresh and green again! 🌸

Without water, plants dry up and die. With water, they grow and bloom!
🌿 How Plants Use Water
🌱Plants drink water through their roots!
🍃Water helps leaves stay green and fresh!
🌸Water helps flowers bloom and grow!
🥕Farmers give water to grow our food — rice, vegetables!
🐄 How Animals Use Water
🐄Cows and buffaloes drink water from rivers and ponds!
🐦Birds drink water — like our thirsty crow!
🐟Fish live inside water — they need it to breathe!
🐘Elephants drink lots of water and love to bathe!
🐕Dogs, cats and all animals need water every day!

🚰 How Tap Water Reaches Us
🌊Water starts in rivers, lakes or underground wells!
🏭It goes to a water treatment plant to become clean!
🏛️Clean water goes to a big overhead water tank!
🚰Then it travels through pipes to reach our taps!
🏠We open the tap — and water comes out! 💧
🌍 Big Ways Water Is Used Outside
🌾Farmers use water in fields to grow rice and crops!
🐄Animals drink from rivers and ponds!
🚌People wash cars, buses and trucks with water!
🏗️Construction workers use water to build houses!
❓ How do we get water through taps?
👉 Ask your elders to know! 🧓💬
Water travels a long way to reach us — we must not waste it! 🙏
