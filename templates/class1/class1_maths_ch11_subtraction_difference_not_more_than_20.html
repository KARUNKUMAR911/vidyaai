📖 What is Subtraction?
When we take away from a group, we subtract! The answer is called the difference! The difference here is not more than 20!

Example: 10 − 6 = 4 (6 out of 10 eggs were used — 4 are left!)

Tens and Units!
What is the TENS? What are the UNITS?
💡 Tens and Units!
After we subtract, we look at the answer!
If the answer is 10 or more → it has a TEN and UNITS!
If the answer is less than 10 → TENS = 0, UNITS = the answer!

Example: 17 − 6 = 11 → 1 TEN, 1 UNIT
Example: 12 − 5 = 7 → 0 TENS, 7 UNITS
